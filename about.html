Our Journey

12th January 2008:

Foundation of Yuva Swabhiman Party

On 12th January 2008, on the occasion of Swami Vivekanandaâ€™s birth anniversary, the Yuva Swabhiman Party was founded with the aim of empowering the youth and creating a more just, inclusive, and progressive society. The party was established with core principles centered around social justice, equality, and youth leadership. Its vision is to address the pressing issues affecting young people today, including education, employment, and political participation. By focusing on these key areas, the Yuva Swabhiman Party seeks to provide a platform where youth voices can be heard and acted upon, ensuring their concerns are addressed in a meaningful way. With a focus on fostering leadership skills and political awareness, it aims to inspire the younger generation to take an active role in shaping the political landscape. 02th February 2011:

World biggest Mass Marriage Ceremony

Shri Ravi Rana made history by arranging a mass wedding that gained recognition in both the Limca Book of Records and the Guinness World Records. On this extraordinary occasion, 3,750 couples were united in a grand multi religious group marriage ceremony. The event, which took place in the city of Amravati, Maharashtra, aimed to promote social harmony and provide a platform for underprivileged couples to start their lives together.
Shri Ravi Rana, known for his charitable work, not only facilitated the weddings but also provided gifts to the newlywed couples to support them as they embarked on their married lives. In an equally remarkable personal turn of events, Ravi Rana himself tied the knot with Sau Navneet Rana, an actress and politician, at the same ceremony. The event not only created a world record but also highlighted Ravi Rana's commitment to social causes, showcasing his ability to bring people together for a larger cause while celebrating love and unity.

Governance:

Work in Local Self-Government Institutions

Gram Panchayat:

In Bhatakuli and Amravati taluka, Yuva Swabhiman Party office-bearers have held the majority of the Sarpanch and Up-Sarpanch positions.

Panchayat Samiti:

Members of the Yuva Swabhiman Party have been elected to the Panchayat Samitis in both Bhattakuli and Amravati, with the positions of Chairman and Vice-Chairman also held by party members.

Zilla Parishad:

Two members from the Yuva Swabhiman Party have been elected to the Zilla Parishad.

Municipal Corporation:

The party contested municipal elections and elected corporators, with two chairperson positions in the municipal corporation i.e. Education and Law & Justice Chairpersons.

District Coordination:

The Yuva Swabhiman Party has successfully established strong coordination with local government bodies across the district, ensuring effective governance and development initiatives.

Statewide Representation:

The party has expanded its reach, gaining recognition in neighboring districts, and has begun to influence state-level politics with representatives securing key positions in regional forums.

Municipality:

The Yuva Swabhiman Party governs the Bhattakuli Municipal Council, with both the Chairman and Municipal President holding office from the party.

COVID-19 Pandemic

Helped the Helpless

Shri Ravi Rana, along with his wife, Navneet Rana, spearheaded initiatives to distribute food, dry rations, and essential supplies to the underprivileged and migrant workers who were most affected by the lockdowns.

Shri Ravi Rana, through his foundation and personal efforts, arranged for financial assistance to support the families of COVID-19 victims, especially those who had lost their breadwinners. He worked to ensure that these families received help quickly and without bureaucratic delays.

Shri Ravi Rana also played an important role in ensuring that his constituency had easy access to COVID-19 vaccinations. He promoted vaccination drives and created awareness about the importance of getting vaccinated to combat the pandemic.

Maha Shiv Puran

A Spiritual Journey

Pandit Pradeepji Mishra Maharaj was the main speaker for the Maha Shiv Puran Katha during this event. His narration of the Maha Shiv Puran captivated thousands of devotees. His ability to explain complex spiritual teachings in an easily understandable manner made the event more impactful and meaningful for the audience.

Shri Ravi Rana and his team, in association with the event, took steps to distribute food, medicines, and essential supplies to the poor and needy people in the community. This was part of the larger goal of combining religious activities with social welfare.

Around 20 lakh people from different parts of Maharashtra and beyond gathered to be a part of the event. The sheer number of attendees showcased the immense popularity of Pandit Pradeepji Mishra Maharaj and his teachings on Lord Shiva through the Maha Shiv Puran.

Shri Hanuman Gadhi

Biggest Hanumanji statue

Shri Hanuman Gadhi is the placed situated near Bhankhed, Amravati. It is primarily famous for the Biggest 111 feet tall statue of Lord Hanumanji.

Shri Ravi Rana played a crucial role in the establishment and development of the Hanuman Gadhi in Amravati, transforming it into a significant religious and cultural landmark. His initiative helped make the temple a popular pilgrimage site for devotees and tourists alike.

To make the temple more accessible, he focused on improving the infrastructure around the temple. This included developing roads, stairs, and other amenities that made it easier for pilgrims to visit and worship at the temple.

Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Statue

Build a symbol of Bravery

Shri Ravi Rana, has taken a remarkable initiative to honor the legacy of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj by building a statue in his memory. The statue is being constructed in Navi Wasti, Badnera, and what makes this gesture even more commendable is that Ravi Rana has undertaken the entire cost of the project from his own salary. This selfless act reflects his deep admiration for Shivaji Maharaj and his commitment to preserving the history and cultural heritage of India.

The statue will serve as a symbol of the bravery, leadership, and wisdom that Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj exhibited during his reign. By funding this project personally, Ravi Rana is not only paying tribute to the great Maratha ruler but also inspiring others to contribute towards the cultural and historical conservation of India..
