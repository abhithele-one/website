    Speech

    At Swabhiman Mahotsav - 2025

    Shri Ravi Rana, a well-known political leader, has always been vocal about his views on social issues, governance, and national progress. In his speech, he often highlights the importance of integrity, transparency, and accountability in leadership. He emphasizes the need for a government that listens to the people and takes swift action in addressing their concerns.

    One of his key points is the role of youth in nation-building, urging the younger generation to be proactive, take responsibility, and contribute to the progress of the country. Rana often stresses the significance of unity in diversity, encouraging citizens to come together despite their differences to build a stronger, more cohesive society. He also advocates for sustainable development, insisting that economic growth should not come at the cost of environmental degradation.

    What We Have Done?

    We have worked to improve community infrastructure, enhance healthcare access, empower youth and women, support farmers, and promote sustainable development. Through education, training, financial support, and resource distribution, we are committed to fostering overall progress and well-being.

    Farmer Development

    Training programs for farmers on modern farming techniques, sustainable and organic practices. Introduced innovative technologies like smart farming tools to improve yields and reduce costs.

    Social Welfare

    Providing financial support or food drives for low-income families. Establishing shelters for the homeless or support for the elderly. Starting initiatives to address mental health awareness and support services.

    Youth Empowerment

    Creating youth mentorship programs and leadership workshops. Establishing scholarship funds or training programs for underprivileged students. Organizing sports and recreational events to keep youth engaged.

    Recent Agitations

    Farmers Protest

    Protest For Farmer

    Farmer suicides continue due to economic hardships, crop failures, and low incomes. The government must ensure a fair Minimum Support Price (MSP) to protect farmers' livelihoods and prevent further tragedies.

    Students Protest

    Protest For Students

    Students are protesting for better education, affordable fees, and improved facilities. They demand equal opportunities, justice, and reforms, urging authorities to address their concerns for a brighter, more inclusive future.

    Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran Protest

    Protest For Water

    Communities unite to demand access to clean water, justice, and sustainability. They raise awareness about the water scarcity crisis, calling for urgent action to protect resources and ensure a fair supply for all.
