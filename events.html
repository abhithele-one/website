Dahi Handi

July 16th, 2023

The largest Dahi Handi in Vidarbha was held in Amravati, where thousands of devotees and participants gathered to celebrate the festival with great enthusiasm. The event featured massive human pyramids attempting to break a high-hanging pot of curd, showcasing community spirit, strength, and devotion in a vibrant display of tradition and joy.

Shiv Mahapuran Katha

December 16th, 2023

The Shiv Mahapurana Katha in Amravati was a 5-day spiritual event where devotees gathered to hear the sacred teachings and stories of Lord Shiva from Shri Pradip Ji Mishra Maharaj. The katha involved discourses on his divine acts, significance, and blessings, creating a profound spiritual atmosphere for worshippers and seekers of wisdom.
